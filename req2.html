Required Documents for

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban (PMAY-U)

Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs

  • Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
  • PAN Card / Voter ID / Driving License (as identity proof)
  • Income Proof (Salary slip, IT return, or self-declaration for EWS/LIG)
  • Proof of family status (marriage certificate, affidavit, etc.)
  • Proof of not owning a pucca house (self-declaration affidavit)
  • Address Proof (Electricity bill, Ration Card, etc.)
  • Bank account details with IFSC code
  • Caste/Minority certificate (if applicable)
