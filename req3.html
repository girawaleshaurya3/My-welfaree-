Required Documents for

Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)

Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

  • Aadhaar Card of applicant (mandatory)
  • BPL Card / SECC-2011 data proof
  • Ration Card
  • Proof of Address (Electricity bill, Aadhaar, etc.)
  • Bank Account details with IFSC code
  • Passport size photographs
  • Caste Certificate (for SC/ST/OBC/Minority/Ex-Servicemen/PH, if applicable)
  • Age proof (for women applicant – must be above 18 years)
